About us

Who we are

The Computer Council of Trinity International College is student organization founded with the intension of providing everything students need to explore their ideas, develop new skills related to the field of computers. We also help in building confidence in students with the help of various programs and programs. This Club consists of computer enthusiasts and technically minded hobbyists as the executive members. The main vision of the club is to expose the students to the latest advancements in computer technology and give individuals an opportunity for sharing and extending ideas, views and knowledge in the field of computers and fast changing technology. Computer and technology has become an inevitable asset of our existence.

Trinity Computer Council (TCC) has been playing active role in aiding the use of information technology. The club focuses on conducting IT related programs, giving platforms to interested people and supervising multiple projects that improves technical as well as social skills. Various computer and technology related competitions are also organized by the club. TCC tries to gather people who are fascinated by the world of technology and wants to explore more about it. The club provides a strong platform for students to practically implement their innovative ideas in computer for different applications. The Club offers indispensible guidance, workshops and organizes various events for students to explore their full potential.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I join the Computer Council?

    You must fill the form published by the ECA department , from where you will be shortlisted for an interview session. Then, the best candidates will be chosen by the Club heads. 

  • What does the Computer Council do ? 

    The Council publishes the annual college magazine, INFINITY. It is also responsible to conduct activities related to science and technology that would help students connect to the digital world. It actively takes part in hosting programs such as web designing and sci-tech.   

  • How can the interested writers send their work ? 

    You can mail it to tcc@trinity.edu.np or send it via our official instagram handle. You can also send it as hardcopy through our club executives. 

  • Who is responsible to publish the magazine?

    The Trinity Computer Council is responsible to publish the magazine in coordination with the college administration.     

  • How can we get associated as a member of the editorial board?

    You can consult with our club members and take in the best suited responsibility for yourself. 

  • What type of creative art form can we submit? 

    You can submit articles, poems, essays, snippets, jokes, sketches, digital arts that showcase your originality and authenticity.
